Frequently Asked Questions

+

What if someone signs up for SlingShot, and you recommend a card I don't have?

SlingShot is committed to sharing the most relevant offers with our users. That means the folks you send our way will be served a plan with a recommendation for the credit card that helps them travel the furthest. In that case, you wouldn't receive a bonus if they sign up.

+

Why should I send my friends to SlingShot, instead of just giving them my referral link?

This frequent flyer stuff is confusing! By sending your friends through SlingShot, you'll get them access to their very own custom plan, rather than just a link to some credit card app. Best of all, it's free - and you'll still earn the bonus miles!

+

Do the people I send to SlingShot have to sign up via my link for me to earn referral bonuses?

Banks and credit card companies track referrals by unique URLs, so to get credit for your friends signing up, they'll need to apply directly via your link.

+

How long does it take for points and miles to credit to my account?

Depending on the program, it can take anywhere from 2-8 weeks for the points from a successful application to process to your account.
